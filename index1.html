Georgia Department of Education · This reference is available to you throughout the test.

Linear Formulas

Slope Formula: $m = \dfrac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1}$

Slope-Intercept Form: $y = mx + b$

Point-Slope Form: $y - y_1 = m(x - x_1)$

Standard Form: $Ax + By = C$

Distance Formula: $d = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}$

Midpoint: $\left(\dfrac{x_1 + x_2}{2}, \dfrac{y_1 + y_2}{2}\right)$

Arithmetic Sequences

Recursive: $a_n = a_{n-1} + d$

Explicit: $a_n = a_1 + (n - 1)d$

Quadratic Formulas

Standard Form: $y = ax^2 + bx + c$

Vertex Form: $y = a(x - h)^2 + k$

Factored Form: $y = a(x - p)(x - q)$

Quadratic Formula: $x = \dfrac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$

Average Rate of Change: change in y divided by change in x over an interval

Exponential Formulas

Exponential Equation: $y = ab^x$

Geometric Recursive: $a_n = r(a_{n-1})$

Geometric Explicit: $a_n = a_1 r^{n-1}$

Compound Interest: $A = P(1 + r)^t$

Statistics Formulas

Mean: $\bar{x} = \dfrac{x_1 + x_2 + \ldots + x_n}{n}$

Interquartile Range (IQR): $IQR = Q_3 - Q_1$

Outliers: More than $1.5 \times IQR$ below $Q_1$ or above $Q_3$

Standard Deviation: Square root of the average of the squared differences from the mean.
